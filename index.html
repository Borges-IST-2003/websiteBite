WHAT IS BITE?

BITE (Best Innovation and Technology Experience) is an engineering competition organized by BEST Lisbon and aimed at college students. Challenging teams (of 3-5 members) to compete for 24 hours in one of two categories: the IT Challenge or the Innovative Design.

This way, each team can choose between can choose between putting their coding skills to test let their creativity take the wheel by designing solutions through CAD (Computer-Aided Design) to address real-world challenges.

During the event, there will be mentorships available which competitors can take advantage of to refine their work and get second opinions on the project.
